What's the bottom line?
We know our business, but we don't exactly know yours, which is great because we strongly believe that great work comes from good relationships and we are convinced that our full potential can only be unleashed when we all work together.
How we are working.
01.
We carry out a conf-call.
Let's get acquainted, learn everything about your project. After the meeting, we will prepare several references and draw up a project structure.

Before we meet with you, look at the questions from our brief.

View the brief
02.
We build communication.
The client has access to chat, task tracker, reports and files in the cloud for his project. All this is available at any time. The client will also have an account manager who will help to understand the reports, and questions about documentation and expenses. At the same time, the team works directly with the client - no manager is needed for this.
03.
We conclude a contract.
We consolidate the main provisions of cooperation. We compose an application for each stage of development, determining the timing and cost. Thus, we can identify and eliminate the main risks, as well as we and you will maintain flexibility in the project. After each completed application, the client receives a report on the work and discusses the progress of the project with the team.

We also allow work without a contract.

Example of a contract
04.
We start prototyping.
We provide the skeleton of the project. You will be able to see how the main blocks and other content will be arranged. For each such stage of development, we assign 2 or more specialists. At this stage, for example, there are two designers.
Example prototype
05.
We develop a concept.
We develop several thematic design options on separate blocks. We approve the concept you like. At the previous and current stage, we admit that requirements may change and ensure close contact between the team and the client. We also use components in the development process.
Concept example
06.
Making edits.
Before sending the layout to the programmers, we check it for errors. We provide a ready-made layout to the client. If necessary, we make changes that are reasonably justified. We work out 3 iterations for free, for the next we issue a separate invoice.
07.
We program.
We write code, develop front-end and back-end. We optimize the site, set up the admin panel, draw up instructions, provide a guarantee and support after the completion of the project.
